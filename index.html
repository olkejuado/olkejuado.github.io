About OHS

Olkejuado High School is a public school located in the Kajiado county of Kenya, on approximately 130 acres of land three kilometres from Kajiado Town. The school provides a well rounded education to approximately 700 students who come from the 47 counties that make up Kenya. OHS started admitting boys in 1978. Before that, it used to be a mixed school. It offers the Kenya Certificate for Secondary Education upon completion of four year courses.

OHS has a strong reputation for excellence in academics, games, music festivals, mathematics contests, and science congresses in the nation. The school has consistently produced top students in the KCSE national examinations, while the music team has been ranked among the top 4 in the national music festivals for ten years in a row now.

The many extracurricular activities offered in school allow our students to cultivate lasting friendships, and fosters independence, determination and strength of character. Our students regularly compete in the National Mathematics contests which challenges them to work to the best of their ability.

Our Learning Community

OHS builds strong relationships among students, teachers, and non-teaching staff through a number of school activities, clubs and societies, sports, among other events. We strive to nurture socially conscious leaders who think beyond their own individual needs, who would use their gifts and intellect to develop their communities.

As a public high school, we challenge our students with a rigorous academic program to prepare them for the demands of higher education. Our students have gone on to excell in the best universities in the world. Our programs are designed to develop a life-long passion for learning among our students.
