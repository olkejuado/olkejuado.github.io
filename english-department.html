Boards & Committees

Volleyball Canada Board of Directors is a policy governing board, which sets policy and hires a Chief Executive Officer to implement policy and the day-to-day work of the organization.
The Board is accountable to the Provincial/Territorial Volleyball Associations (Active Members).
