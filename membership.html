Membership

Volleyball Canada is committed to creating a unified “Volleyball in Canada” family. The strategic goal is to build a system where every person involved with volleyball in Canada is connected to the sport’s governing body.

If you looking for our VRS (Volleyball Registration System), please click here.

Benefits & Categories

Benefits of aligned membership

  • Harmonized categories of membership across Canada and link them to stages of long term athlete development (LTAD).
  • Improved communication and interaction among Volleyball Canada and all its PTA partners.
  • Improved alignment of athlete and coach development systems within volleyball through affiliation with scholastic, post-secondary, adult recreation and masters volleyball segments in addition to the youth competitive segment.
  • Alignment of marketing and promotional efforts by communicating and elevating the Volleyball Canada brand. 

Participant Categories

  • Youth Competitive Player
  • Adult Player
  • Post-Secondary Player
  • Leaders - Club/Team Personnel, Referees
  • Youth Development Player
  • Leader
  • Recreational Player
  • Leader - Volunteer
  • Elementary and High School Player
